But We Need More...

The margin of victory gives an idea of not just consitency and longetivity but also dominance
  • The bar chart below shows the average margin of victory for all the teams that have won
  • The selection box can be used to switch between the two possible methods of victory, by runs or by wickets
  • No bar indicates that the country has not won under that particular mode of victory
  • Hovering over each bar gives additional information on number of games won
  • Margin of runs seems to be a better metric of dominance under which, Afghanistan and Wet indies seem to excel
